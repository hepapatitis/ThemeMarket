Welcome to BF-Calc (a.k.a Brave Frontier Calculator)

Greetings

Hello and welcome to Brave Frontier Calculator! First of all, thank you very much for coming to this page and using the calculator. This website is a fan made page that is created to help Brave Frontier players based on Brave Frontier Wikia. Be sure to check the Wiki too!

BF-Calc is fan-made tools to help Brave Frontier players to focus on their game and leave the trivial calculations to us. The current existing calculators are EXP Calculator, Team Unit Cost Calculator, Metal Calculator, and Zel Calculator.

I hope you guys find this tool useful. If you have any question, suggestion or feedback, feel free to send me an email at bravefrontier@memoriesdust.com or send me a query by form.
